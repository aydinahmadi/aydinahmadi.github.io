I graduated from Koç University in Computer Engineering and Science. My research interests include Robotics, Model-Based Control, Reinforcement Learning, Perception, and Long-Horizon Planning. I focus on developing algorithms that enable robots to learn task-agnostic and task-specific behaviors, integrating planning, perception, and control methods such as MPC, Reinforcement Learning, and deep learning for real-world applications. I also explore grasping, navigation, and in-hand manipulation.

During my studies, I gained experience in Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision with Deep Learning, and Embodied Intelligence using tools such as Stable Baselines, OpenAI Gym, Hugging Face models, PyTorch, and TensorFlow. I worked in the KUIS AI Lab under the supervision of Prof. Barış Akgün, developing model-based approaches for planar object manipulation with robotic systems like the Franka Emika Panda and ROS.
