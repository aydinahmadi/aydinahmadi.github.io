  • Comparing DDPG and SAC for Robotics Tasks
    Compared the performance of Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG) and Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) algorithms for robotic tasks. Implementations from the Stable-Baselines framework were contrasted with custom code in the PandaGym environment, focusing on pushing and reaching tasks.

    Investigated the efficacy of augmenting the DDPG algorithm with Hindsight Experience Replay (HER) and Prioritized Experience Replay (PER) to improve learning performance. Compared a basic DDPG approach with sparse rewards against a modified version employing HER + PER on PandaGym tasks.
    (Embodied Intelligence Course)
